Hello!
I'm Bianca

I have been living in Costa Rica for 12 years because of my love for nature and life in the ocean. Traveling was always second nature to me and has become my life's purpose. I started shooting real estate in 2018 in Costa Rica. This experience has helped me grow as a travel content creator. As a surfer, I started enjoying water photography and in 2022, I decided to take a freediving course and thats when I felt like I found my soul's purpose. I believe freediving and underwater photography is what sets me apart from most other content creators. I am very comfortable in water both infront and behind the camera. I was recently published as a storyteller at Ocean Culture Life and will continue pursuing underwater photography and conservation efforts.

As a travel influencer, photographer,  videographer and model, I offer my services to anyone in the travel industry. I provide high quality images and videos with unique perspectives of aerial and underwater view points as well as land. 

Content Creation - Creating unique content professionally for your brand, hotel, product, or service with punctual deliverables.

Social Media & Brand Promotion - Sharing your brand or services with my community of travel minded followers..

Long Term Brand Partnerships - Typically people need seven points of contact before they make a purchase, which is why long term brand partnerships are the best way to really resonate with people. I can work closely with you on a 6-12+ month partnership to make sure your message is correctly delivered to our followers and your message gets heard loud and clear!

Modelling - As an adventure seeker, travel addict, and mermaid, I believe I can offer a unique perspective as a model for your brand. I have just been contacted for an underwater casting role and hope to continue to help brands showcase their products around the world.
