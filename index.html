Hello!

I am Bianca

Bianca has been living in Costa Rica for 12 years because of her love for nature and life in the ocean. Originally from Chicago with Greek/Syrian heritage, travelling was always second nature. In 2022, discovered freediving and underwater photography which now sets her apart from most other content creators. She was recently published as a storyteller at Ocean Culture Life and will continue pursuing underwater photography.
